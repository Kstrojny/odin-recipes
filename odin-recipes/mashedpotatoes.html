Mega Mashed Potatoes

When I was a child, I loved going to all you can eat buffets so that I could create this beautiful piece. I forget the unique and original name I gave this creation and there's no correct imgaes online that I can find but for this recipe, I will be calling the creation "Mega Mashed Potatoes".

Ingredients

  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Canned Corn
  • Brown Gravy
  • French Fries
  • Fried Chicken for the meal but is optional
  • Grated Cheese

Instructions

  1. Plop your mashed potatoes next to your fried chicken
  2. Mix in the canned corn, as much as you like to as little as you like
  3. Smother her in gravy
  4. Top her off with some french fries and grated cheese

Tips

  • This was created by my child mind. Have fun with it!
